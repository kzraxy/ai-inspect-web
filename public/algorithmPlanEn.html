Algorithm Optimization Program

Welcome to the "Algorithm Optimization Program." In order to improve our algorithm products, enhance service quality, and improve the user experience of our products, we need to collect certain information and materials provided by you. Through these image data, we optimize our algorithm products to continuously improve their user experience and operational performance, specifically enhance algorithm accuracy, and launch new products and services that are more helpful to you.

Please read the specific content of the "Algorithm Optimization Plan" carefully and decide for yourself whether to join based on your preference. The specific details of the Algorithm Optimization Plan are as follows:

1. INFORMATION COLLECTION

Under this user experience program, the information we need to collect includes:

  • 1. The images you submit, including but not limited to the voice files you provide when using the speech recognition and speech synthesis functions.
  • 2. We promise that this plan will never involve any of your personal information that has not been legally disclosed, including your name, address, email, financial account, ID number, phone number, etc. This plan does not require you to register, nor will it obtain any other personal information from you. However, please note that the name of the legal representative included in the business license image is considered legally disclosed personal information.

2. INFORMATION USAGE

After collecting your relevant image materials, we may use the aforementioned information ourselves or through our affiliated companies to optimize algorithm training, and use the training results in one or more of the following ways:

  • (1) Improve the functionality and user experience of algorithm products through training results, enhancing the efficiency and accuracy of algorithm recognition;
  • (2) Optimize business usage scenarios to improve the accuracy of text and speech recognition;
  • (3) Use for marketing and promotion of algorithm products, for example, stating the amount of data used for product training externally, without disclosing any personal information;
  • (4) Other application activities based on statistical analysis results.

3. INFORMATION SECURITY

Your information security is very important to us. To this end:

  • 1. We will review the practices and methods of information collection, transmission, storage, and processing (including physical security measures) to prevent unauthorized access to various systems.
  • 2. We use HTTPS to encrypt data transmission services to ensure the security of your information during transmission.
  • 3. Images are stored in encrypted form, and the URLs have a time limit when accessed, ensuring the security of both data storage and access.
  • 4. We will continue to strive to protect the security of your information and implement measures such as full-process encryption for storage and transmission to prevent your information from being accessed, used, or disclosed without authorization.
